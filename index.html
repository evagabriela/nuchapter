Who are we?

Alpha Psi Lambda was established on February 11, 1985 at The Ohio State University and has grown to 22 chapters in 5 states with over 2,100 members.

Being a brotherhood and sisterhood we believe in the concept Familia to create a support network for our members. Our fraternity promotes academic achievement, leadership development and cultural awareness.

Our Nu chapter was found on 2001 by 6 founders at the University of Toledo. Although we are a Latino interest fraternity, Alpha Psi Lambda and our Nu Chapter has welcomed people from a broad spectrum of cultures including, Latin American, Caribbean, European, Asian, and American. Alpha Psi Lambda stands as one family united. Our strength lies in our ability to focus on our culture while appreciating the diversity of others.
